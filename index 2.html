Behind the Magic

Every great experience has a story behind it. Discover how we transformed constraints into creativity, turning a single dorm room into multiple worlds through the power of perspective and imagination.

The Creative Process

Constraint as Catalyst: Limited to a single dorm room, we discovered that constraints fuel creativity rather than limit it. Every angle, every lighting choice, every sound became intentional and meaningful.

Sound as Storyteller: Each knock was meticulously crafted and recorded to convey specific emotions and urgency levels. The rhythm, intensity, and spacing tell the story before you even see who's behind the door.

Performance in Miniature: Working in close quarters demanded precise choreography and authentic performances. Every gesture and expression had to be perfect because there was nowhere to hide imperfection.

Technology Meets Art: We combined traditional filmmaking techniques with modern interactive design, creating an experience that bridges the gap between passive viewing and active participation.
